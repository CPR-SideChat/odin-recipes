Homemade Popcorn

Store bought popcorn has become a home staple for most households; but nothing beats homemade popcorn that goes straight to the heart.

Ingredients

  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil (45 mL)
  • Popcorn Kernels (80 mL)
  • Unsalted Butter (15 mL)
  • Salt to Taste

Steps

  1. Heat the Oil: Heat the extra virgin olive oil in a 1 L thick-bottomed saucepan turned to medium-high heat.
  2. Put 3 or 4 kernels in the oil to see when they pop.
  3. Once they pop, add the rest of the kernels to have an even layer covering the saucepan.
  4. Cover the pot, remove from heat and count approximately 30 seconds.
  5. Return saucepan to medium-high heat. Shimmy the pan to shake the kernels, gently shaking them.
  6. Once the amount of kernels popping slows to a few seconds between popping, remove saucepan from heat.
  7. Dump popped corn into a wide bowl.
  8. Reuse saucepan and melt butter in the empty hot pan.
  9. Pour the melted butter evenly over all the popped kernels in the wide bowl.
  10. Sprinkle salt over popcorn to taste.
  11. Serve!
